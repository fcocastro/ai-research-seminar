About the series

AI is increasingly contributing not only to productivity but to progress on longstanding scientific and mathematical problems. Many of us are beginning to integrate it into our workflows in different ways, and this series is a place to compare notes. It is interactive and community-driven; our aim is to learn from each other as the landscape evolves.
